# people/ — the people you care about

Not just "family". Partner, parents, kids, housemates, close friends,
long-term friends, mentors, students, internet friends, pets … whoever
matters to you, whoever needs your attention right now, gets a place here.

## What goes here

- **Key relationship files**: a short note per important person (age /
  health / stage / how the relationship is going)
- **Family decisions**: care arrangements, education plans, household
  division of labor
- **Partner / marriage**: shared goals, recent important things,
  relationship maintenance notes
- **Parents / elders**: health checklist, insurance, kid-side contacts,
  important dates
- **Kids** (if any): growth records, school stuff, health
- **Friends / circles**: long-term close friends' situations, things you
  share
- **Insurance / legal**: people-related policies, trusts, wills
- **Important dates**: birthdays / anniversaries / major surgery dates
  / move dates

## What this looks like at different life stages

| Your situation | Roughly what's here |
|----------------|---------------------|
| Student / living alone | Parents brief + a few close friends + housemate (if any) |
| Long distance relationship / marriage | Partner profile + maintenance cadence + shared important dates |
| Married with kids | Partner + kids + both sets of parents + childcare division |
| Sandwich generation | Parents (care) + kids (education) + yourself (in the middle) — Muse will give advice that spans them |
| Single / childfree | Parents + chosen family + long-term friend circle |
| Retired / older | Spouse + adult children + old friends + medical contacts |
| Divorced / blended family | Complex graph → use a single "relationship map" md file |

## What Muse can do with this

- **Cross-domain decisions**: connect family info + your finances + your
  time (e.g. "parents' surgery + this year's cash flow + your vacation
  days" evaluated together)
- **Care planning**: prompts for what to screen for / prepare for at
  the parent or elder's stage
- **Insurance review**: gaps / overlaps / expirations
- **Relationship maintenance**: important-date reminders + personalized
  suggestions (combined with their recent situation)
- **Major decisions** (having a kid / moving house / parents relocating
  / long-distance reunion): list affected dimensions, preconditions,
  kill criteria

## Notes

- **This is the most sensitive directory**. Strongly recommend full-disk
  encryption on the whole muselab archive and **do not** sync it to
  public clouds
- Information about others: writing "M" / "father" / "housemate A"
  instead of real names is fine — what matters is that Muse knows the
  relationship
- Health info should be per-person subdirectories, clearly named
- Do **not** write SSNs / national IDs / bank accounts here — those go
  in a password manager
- Divorce / legal-dispute materials: consider a separate encrypted
  file, don't leave it in the open

## Getting started

1. Write a paragraph about "who or what relationship I care about most
   right now, and how they're doing"
2. List 3-5 of the most important people in your life (one sentence each)
3. Write **the one relationship problem you most want to solve right now**

Muse will give tailored advice from this starting point.
